I am a PhD Candidate at MIT and CSAIL. My supervisor is Prof. Fox Harrell and I work at the Imagination, Computation and Expression Lab. In my research, I adopt a cross-disciplinary approach to the design and development of computational technologies that enable us to understand digital media systems and their users better.
