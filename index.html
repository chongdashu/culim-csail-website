Chong-U Lim

I am a PhD Candidate at MIT and CSAIL. My supervisor is Prof. Fox Harrell and I work at the Imagination, Computation and Expression Lab. In my research, I adopt a cross-disciplinary approach to the design and development of computational technologies that enable us to understand digital media systems and their users better. This incorporates theory and techniques from computer science, cognitive science, social science and the humanities. More broadly, my research interests span:

  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Human-Computer Interaction and Design
  • Computational Intelligence and Video Games
